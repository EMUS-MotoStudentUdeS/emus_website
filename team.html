Who we are

Students building a competitive electric prototype

Multidisciplinary students in mechanical, electrical, controls, software, and business collaborate to design, build, and test a fully electric race bike for MotoStudent 2025.

We mix classroom theory with track-ready engineering: from chassis and battery design to testing and race operations.
