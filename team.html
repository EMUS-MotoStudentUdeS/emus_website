Team

Engineering students building electric performance

EMUS is an engineering student team from Universite de Sherbrooke building a fully electric race bike for MotoStudent 2027.

Who we are

Students building a competitive electric prototype

Multidisciplinary students in mechanical, electrical, controls, software, and business collaborate to design, build, and test a fully electric race bike for MotoStudent 2027.

We mix classroom theory with track-ready engineering: from chassis and battery design to testing and race operations.
