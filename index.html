About MotoStudent

Design. Build. Race.

MotoStudent is an international engineering competition where university teams design and build a race motorcycle prototype, evaluated at MotorLand Aragón (Alcañiz, Spain). Categories include Petrol and Electric.

EMUS is competing in the Electric category, bringing together students from the Université de Sherbrooke to engineer a fully electric race bike for MotoStudent 2027.

Competition timeline

  • Design + validation

    Now · System architecture, validation, supplier alignment

  • Build

    2025–2027 · Prototype manufacturing and testing

  • Final event
