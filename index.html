About MotoStudent

Design. Build. Race.

MotoStudent is an international engineering competition where university teams design and build a race motorcycle prototype, evaluated at MotorLand Aragon (Alcaniz, Spain). Categories include Petrol and Electric.

EMUS is competing in the Electric category, bringing together students from the Universite de Sherbrooke to engineer a fully electric race bike for MotoStudent 2027.
